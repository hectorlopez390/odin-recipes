Arroz Con Leche

Description

Arroz Con Leche or rice pudding is a dish made from rice mixed with water or milk and other ingredients such as cinnamon, vanilla, and raisins. The dish can be found around the world with many varying recipes.This particular recipe and style comes from Mexico, but may also be found to have varying ingredients throughout Mexico.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 4-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 12-ounce can evaporated milk
  • 1 14-ounce can condensed milk
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Ground cinnamon, for dusting

Steps

  1. Put the water, rice, and cinnamon stick in a medium-size heavy saucepan set over medium-high heat
  2. Bring to a boil, uncovered, and cook until the rice is tedner, about 18 minutes
  3. Strain out the liquid, discard the cinnamon and reserve the rice
  4. Return the rice to the saucepan. Stir in the evaporated milk, condensed milk, and whole milk
  5. Continue cooking over medium-high heat until the mixture comes to a boil
  6. Reduce the heat to low and cook, uncovered, stirring constantly, until the mixture is thick, about 20 minutes
  7. Transfer the pudding to a serving bowl
  8. Dust the top of the pudding with ground cinnamon, serve, and enjoy!
